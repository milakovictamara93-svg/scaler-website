01Doctrine

One filter for everything we make

The brand never claims. It counts.

Copy

No sentence ships that couldn't survive an audit. Real numbers, plain verbs, periods. If it sounds like a promise, rewrite it as a measurement.

Image

If we didn't shoot it or measure it, it doesn't appear. No stock, no generated faces, no decorative illustration on marketing surfaces.

Design

Black and white do the work. One accent, used once. Space is confidence; a crowded layout is a nervous one.

02Logo

Two colours. Two placements. Nothing else.

Black on light. White on dark. The mark is never colourised, never translucent, never set on a busy photograph without a protective plate. Twelve squares, four triangles, one 30° chamfer — the same chamfer builds every brand shape, so shapes and logo never share a surface.

Combination law. Any colour may sit on white or black. Colours never sit on other colours. Background grey never sits on a colour. One colour per surface — a second accent is a second opinion.

Legibility law. Aspect colours appear only when the content is literally about that category. Emission yellow and ESG green never carry text at any size. Energy red carries text at 18px and above, on white only.

About us

Clear data, financial insight, long-term thinking.

Our team brings deep experience in finance, real estate, and technology. We built Scaler to make complex ESG data simple and useful, so you can move faster, make better decisions, and create real impact.

What sets us apart is our focus on what really matters: clear data, financial insight, and long-term thinking. We're here to help you navigate challenges and reach your goals with confidence.

Why Scaler

Built for this sector, on purpose.

The spark

In 2021, we saw real estate struggling with outdated tools and scattered data. The need for a simpler, smarter way to manage ESG and performance was clear. That's why we created Scaler — to simplify complexity and help teams make smarter decisions.

Why real estate

Real estate faces significant long-term risks but also shapes how we live and work every day. Scaler is built specifically for this sector, giving real estate professionals the tools they need to take confident action.

The bigger picture

We believe long-term value and responsible action go hand in hand. Scaler helps teams make decisions that deliver today's results and build tomorrow's resilience.
